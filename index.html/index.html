"Rather than deliberately trying to draw something, use something you yourself like and want to draw, and I think the characters that come out of that will really have their own individuality."
-Akira Toriyama

Akira Toriyama is a Japanese manga artist, creator and character designer known for his unique art style and storytelling. His ability to blend action, humor and adventure is the reason why he is beloved by many all around the world. His work had an immense impact on the world of manga, anime, and gaming.
